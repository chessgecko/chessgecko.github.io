What we do
Muscle Metrics is a company built around modernizing the gym-going experience. We are creating a future where workouts are automatically recorded and the statistics are uploaded to a users profile automatically. The statistics are then displayed in an easy to understand and beautiful format that will allow the user to see what is going well and when they should change things up. This is a revolutionary way not only for lifters to improve themselves but also for trainers to make incredibly accurate suggestions. It is also a great way for a gym to see what is in demand, what equipment is worth investing in and what can go.

Effortlessly collect fitness data

Using bluetooth in smartphones and in our chips we can effortlessly send the statistics from a piece of gym equipment to a user's phone

Help people utilize their data

The data is then sent from the phone to our website where customers can see their personal data laid out an a beautiful and intuitive manner. Gyms can also use this data to collect usage statistics, and make educated decisions about the type of equipment they should invest in.

Train more Efficiently

This data will be able to allow trainers to tailor routines and advice for each person on a whole new level. No more wasting time lifting inefficiently
